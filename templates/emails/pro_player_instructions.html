Thank you for joining us as a valued partner in the early rollout of BowlersNetwork.

To ensure you have a smooth onboarding experience, we’ve outlined a simple step-by-step roadmap to help you get started and explore the platform with confidence:

  1. Access The Welcome Link
    Begin by clicking the link shared with you in the welcome email.
  2. Create Your Account
    Set up your username and password. This will serve as your main login for the partner portal.
  3. Complete The Quick Survey
    After signing up, you’ll be redirected to a short survey. This helps us understand your usage and tailor the experience as we continue improving the platform.
  4. Update Your Profile
    Head over to Settings to update your profile details. A complete profile helps us provide better support and ensures smoother interactions within the platform.
  5. Engage Through the Feed
    From the Feed, you can post updates, share insights, and engage with other members. This will be a core part of your platform experience.
  6. Explore The Full Side Panel
    Use the side panel to browse through all available features. We encourage you to explore — it’s the best way to get familiar with everything the platform offers.

If you need any assistance or run into issues at any point, please feel free to reach out. We’re here to support you throughout the onboarding journey.

Thank you again for being an early partner. Your involvement is important to us, and we look forward to growing together.

Warm Regards,
The BowlersNetwork Team
